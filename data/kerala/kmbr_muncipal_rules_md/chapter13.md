## 91. Conversion of roof and shutters in existing biiilding

**Citation:** Chapter 13, Rule 91
**Source:** `chapter13.html#chapter13-1`

91.
Conversion of roof and shutters in existing biiilding.- Notwithstanding
anything contained in these rules, the Secretary, may permit the conversion
of thatched roof with tile or asbestos or cement sheet or light sheet
or tin sheet and vice versa and conversion of wooden shutters with
rolling shutters or rolling grills or collapsible shutters or to install
the same in any building existing at tile commencement of these rules
withoutany structural change or area increase.
